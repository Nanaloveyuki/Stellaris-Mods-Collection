EXOTIC WEAPONS 1.0 - DAMAGE AND VANILLA COMPARISON GUIDE

========================================================

PURPOSE
-------

This guide compares the Exotic Weapons components with broadly similar vanilla components. It is intended
to show their approximate power, not to predict exact fleet-combat results.


HOW THE NUMBERS ARE CALCULATED
------------------------------

Average hit damage = (minimum damage + maximum damage) / 2
Raw DPS            = average hit damage / cooldown
Accuracy DPS       = raw DPS x accuracy

The main comparison table uses raw DPS so that weapon accuracy remains visible as a separate balancing factor. It does
not apply shield, armor, or hull multipliers. It also does not include tracking versus evasion, missile interception,
range advantage, firing arcs, minimum range, overkill, target selection, or repeatable technologies.

Important interpretation notes:

  * Penetration can be worth far more than raw DPS because penetrated defense layers are skipped.
  * Shield/armor/hull multipliers change DPS only while that defense layer is being attacked.
  * Torpedo size_damage_factor is not included in raw DPS. Cyclonic Torpedo has 1.50 and Black Arrow has 1.00, compared
    with 0.20 on vanilla Devastator Torpedoes, so their anti-large-target advantage is even greater than the table shows.
  * Missile DPS assumes the projectile reaches its target. Point defense can reduce real missile damage substantially.
  * Mjolnir damage ranges from 1 to 800. Its average is high, but individual shots are extremely inconsistent.
  * Langston Field Projector is a defensive auxiliary component and therefore has no weapon DPS.

QUICK BALANCE SUMMARY
---------------------

Relatively close to vanilla (+/- 30% raw DPS):
  Thanix Cannon, MAC Accelerator, Asgard Plasma Beam, Phaser Array, Necron Gauss Flayer, Gridfire Projector,
  Superlaser Focusing Array, Bolo Hellbore, Bifrost Lance, Gungnir Spear Launcher.

Clearly stronger than comparable vanilla weapons (roughly +35% to +180% raw DPS):
  Wave-Motion Gun, Molecular Disruptor, PDC Network, Sonic Disruption Array, Dalek Extermination Ray,
  Emerald Construct Cannon, Omega Beam, Stormbringer Soul Blade, Dragonfire Cannon, Mjolnir Arc Projector,
  Medusa Gaze Array.

Extreme outliers by vanilla slot efficiency:
  Cyclonic Torpedo, Epstein-Drive Torpedo, Molecular Detachment Device, Shardblade Projector,
  Black Arrow Missile, Excalibur Psionic Blade.

The four G-slot weapons deserve particular attention in playtesting. Their raw DPS is approximately 13 to 23 times
their selected vanilla references before Cyclonic/Black Arrow size scaling is applied. If the intended design is
"powerful but still near vanilla," these should be the first components reduced. If the intended design is a heroic
power-fantasy collection, the current numbers communicate that identity very clearly.
