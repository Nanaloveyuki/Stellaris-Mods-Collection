VIRAL WEAPONS DAMAGE GUIDE
==========================

PURPOSE
-------
This guide compares the current Viral Weapons statistics with their closest
vanilla equivalents. It is intended to help players decide whether a Viral or
vanilla component is more appropriate for a ship design.

The values below describe the mod as currently configured. They are not a
statement of intended balance.


HOW THE NUMBERS ARE CALCULATED
------------------------------
Average damage per shot = (minimum damage + maximum damage) / 2
Raw DPS                 = average damage / cooldown
Listed-hit DPS          = raw DPS x listed accuracy

Listed-hit DPS does not account for the target's evasion. Tracking counters
target evasion, so high-tracking weapons perform better than the table implies
against corvettes, destroyers, missiles, and strike craft.

Damage multipliers are applied only when damage reaches the relevant defensive
layer. Unless otherwise stated, these weapons do not bypass shields or armor.


VIRAL CORROSION
---------------
Successful hits from the S, M, L, X, and T Viral weapons apply Viral Corrosion.
Point defense and Viral Interceptors do not currently apply it.

  Duration:                 15 days
  Maximum stacks:           10
  Shield capacity penalty:  -2% per stack
  Armor capacity penalty:   -2% per stack
  Maximum penalty:          -20% shield and armor capacity

Each additional successful hit advances the effect by one tier and refreshes
the 15-day duration. Stellaris 4.4.x does not expose shield/armor hardness as a
valid ship modifier, so the implemented mechanic reduces capacity instead.

Corrosion is most valuable in sustained fleet combat where several Viral
weapons can repeatedly hit the same large target. It is less valuable when a
target dies before multiple stacks accumulate.

Practical choice:

* Choose Viral weapons for substantially higher sustained damage, longer range,
  higher tracking, good shield damage, and Corrosion.
* Choose Plasma when individual high-damage volleys matter or when using a
  strictly vanilla-balanced fleet.
* Plasma remains more specialized against armor per point of base damage, but
  the current Viral fire rate overwhelms that advantage in total DPS.
* Under the current values, Viral S/M/L weapons are not sidegrades. They are
  considerably stronger than Plasma Cannon III in most ordinary engagements.

Weapon                         Avg hit  Cooldown  Raw DPS  Accuracy  Tracking  Range
-----------------------------  -------  --------  -------  --------  --------  -----
Viral Point Defense               5.50      0.80     6.88       80%       80%     35
Guardian Point-Defense III         6.00      5.00     1.20       75%       30%     30

Listed-hit DPS:

  Viral Point Defense:             5.50
  Guardian Point-Defense III:      0.90

Both can target missiles and strike craft. Viral Point Defense has much higher
fire rate, tracking, range, and expected DPS. It is the stronger general P-slot
choice under the current configuration. It does not apply Viral Corrosion.

------------------------------------------------
Both components launch eight craft.

---------------------------  ------------------  ----------------
Damage per craft                  5-13                6-17

Accuracy                           95%                100%
Tracking                           85%                100%

Health per craft                  30                  12
Armor per craft                   15                   0
Shield per craft                   0                  25
Evasion                           75%                 80%
Regeneration per day              0.15                0.50
Engagement range                 150                 125
Configured speed                   9                 700

Damage behavior:

  Viral Interceptors:  1.20x shields, 1.50x armor, 1.10x hull
  Strike Craft III:    bypasses shields and deals 1.50x armor damage

Practical choice:

* Choose vanilla Strike Craft III for higher wing DPS, perfect tracking, shield
  bypass, much faster replacement, and vastly higher configured craft speed.
* Choose Viral Interceptors for longer engagement range and a tougher individual
  health/armor profile.
* The Viral craft speed value of 9 is dramatically below vanilla's 700. Until
  rebalanced, this may cause poor travel and interception performance.
* Viral Interceptors do not apply Viral Corrosion.

QUICK RECOMMENDATIONS
---------------------

Against corvettes/destroyers:
  Viral S and M weapons benefit from their high tracking. Viral Point Defense
  is also unusually effective against missiles and strike craft.

Against cruisers/battleships/titans:
  Viral L, X, and T weapons combine high sustained DPS with rapidly accumulating
  Corrosion. Focused fire gives the best result.

For opening-volley artillery:
  Tachyon Lances and Perdition Beams retain much higher alpha damage. Vanilla
  weapons may destroy a target before Viral weapons realize their sustained-DPS
  advantage.

For carrier fleets:
  Vanilla Strike Craft III is currently the safer damage choice. Viral
  Interceptors are durable and engage farther away, but their very low configured
  speed and slow regeneration are serious disadvantages.

For a vanilla-like balance level:
  Prefer vanilla weapons. Current Viral direct-fire and point-defense values are
  significantly above their closest vanilla equivalents.
